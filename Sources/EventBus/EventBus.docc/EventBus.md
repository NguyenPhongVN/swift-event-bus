# ``EventBus``

Type-safe publish/subscribe primitives for Swift apps.

## Overview

`EventBus` supports:

- Closure-based subscriptions with `on` and `off`
- One-shot async waits with `next`
- Streaming with `AsyncStream`
- Combine publishers
- SwiftUI integration with `@EventListener`, `@EventPublisher`, and `.eventBus(_:)`
- Middleware, metrics, and replay
